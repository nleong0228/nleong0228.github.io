Noah Leong

Producer, Developer, and Multi-Instrumentalist

About

Icon

Background

A student at the Berklee College of Music, Noah hails from the Detroit metro area, and currently resides in Boston, MA. Growing up as a classicaly trained violist, with an interest in technology. Noah has used tech in music from a young age.

Icon

Developer

Through his studies at the College Noah has taken classes like Audio Programming in C, Programming in Swift, and Machine Learning for Musicians. In these classes Noah has created a wide variety of new musical devices and programs to expand his performances.

Icon

Producer

Noah has worked on tracks as a beatmaker and mixing/mastering engineer. With experience both playing and behind the board, Noah has a thorough understanding of recording workflow both in the studio, and remote.

Icon

String Arranger

Through his experience as a string player from a young age, Noah has been able to extend his palatability by writing and recording string arrangements. As a violinist/violist Noah is able to record full string arrangements himself.

Icon

Performance

Through his classical background, Noah has led many ensembles as both principal violist and soloist. In addition, Noah currently explores the use of the Viola in jazz, and is also a bassist and guitarist that has played in both jazz ensembles and recording projects.

Icon

Game Audio & Post Production

Through his studies at Berklee, Noah has been able to work in post production in a variety of projects. Whether it be sound design for linear movies, or game audio implementation with Wwise and Unity Noah has a wide variety of skills in post production.
