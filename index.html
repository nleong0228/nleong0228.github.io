About Me

Hails from the Detroit metro area, currently residing in Boston, a Professional and multi faceted musician, with a passionate interest in a wide range of styles including Jazz, Classical, Hip-Hop, RnB, and Pop. Has extensive knowledge of sound design techniques and an interest in coding and computer music. Also has spent a vast amount of time in the studio through recording sessions, both playing and behind the board. Thorough understanding of music theory, and extensive performance experience. Has created and produced numerous original tracks, and arranged/recorded entire string ensemble parts for many projects. Additionally, has taught private lessons and workshops for Jazz and Classical string playing.
