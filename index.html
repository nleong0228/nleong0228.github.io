ABOUT

Noah Leong

Noah hails from the Detroit metro area, and currently resides in Boston, MA. A professional and multi faceted musician, with a passionate interest in a wide range of styles including Jazz, Classical, Hip-Hop, RnB, and Pop. Noah has extensive knowledge of sound design techniques and an interest in coding and computer music. Through these combined focuses, his more recent work involves working with embedded devices such as the Arduino and Bela to create interactive touch interfaces. In addition Noah also has experience with circuit design for analog synthesizers and effects boxes. He also has spent a vast amount of time in the studio through recording sessions, both playing and behind the board. With a thorough understanding of music theory, and extensive performance experience, he has created and produced numerous original tracks, and arranged/recorded entire string ensemble parts for many projects. Additionally, Noah has taught private lessons and workshops for Jazz and Classical string playing.

This project is a rescore of a Motorola Droid commercial. In addition to composing the score, all recordings and arrangements were done by myself, including the string parts.
